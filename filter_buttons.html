The Rutgers ClubAccepts Meal Plans!

Located at 199 College Ave. is The Rutgers Club. Every Monday and Thursday is Student Night where a student can enjoy a buffet dinner with a meal swipe and $2. The buffet consists of soup of the day, pasta entree, vegetable, starch, protein entree, vegetarian entree and marinated cold salads, fresh field green salad, fresh rolls, dessert station and soda.

Reservations are required. The first reservation is at 5 pm and the last is at 7:30 pm. Call (848) 932-7139 before 11:30 am or after 2:30 pm to make reservations. E-mail reservations are not accepted.
